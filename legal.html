Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

The mlOS Foundation ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the mlOS software, visit our website, or interact with our services.

Key Point: mlOS is open-source software that runs locally on your systems. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data from your ML models, inference requests, or runtime operations.

Information We Collect

Website Visitors: When you visit mlosfoundation.org, we may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (page views, referrer information) via privacy-respecting analytics
  • Information you voluntarily provide through contact forms or newsletter signups
  • Technical information such as browser type and operating system for compatibility purposes

mlOS Software: The mlOS runtime and kernel module:

  • Do not phone home or transmit any telemetry data
  • Do not collect information about your models or inference workloads
  • Do not require an internet connection to operate
  • Store all data locally on your systems

GitHub and Open Source

When you interact with our GitHub repositories, GitHub's privacy policy applies to that interaction. We may see publicly available information such as usernames associated with issues, pull requests, and contributions.

How We Use Information

Information collected through our website is used to:

  • Improve website content and user experience
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Send project updates to newsletter subscribers (opt-in only)
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve documentation

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any personal information we collect. The mlOS software itself is designed with security in mind, operating entirely within your infrastructure without external data transmission.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the maintainers through the channels listed there.

Patents

Last updated: January 2025

Patent Applications

The mlOS Foundation has filed provisional patent applications to protect key innovations in the project. These patents cover novel approaches to ML runtime execution, memory management, and kernel-level optimizations.

US-63/861,527

Predictive Memory Management for ML Inference

Methods and systems for predicting memory requirements of ML models and proactively managing memory allocation to minimize latency and prevent out-of-memory conditions during inference.

Provisional Application Filed
US-63/865,176

Kernel-Native ML Runtime Architecture

Architecture and methods for executing ML inference workloads with kernel-level integration, enabling deterministic scheduling, reduced context switching, and hardware-optimized execution paths.
